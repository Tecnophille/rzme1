Rezime AI Resume Builder FAQs



I am unable to access my account.

Please attempt the following steps:

  1. Attempt logging in using your social media account or google account.
  2. Disable any browser extensions that may be active.
  3. Clear the cache in your browser.
  4. Refresh the page to see if the issue persists.
  5. Use the incognito or private mode in your browser to log in.
  6. If you have used Rezime more than a year ago, your documents may be saved on our legacy builder. To access Classic Rezime, please click on "My Account" in the top right corner and utilize the login option on the left side.

Is it necessary to tailor my resume for each job application?

Yes! You absolutely should! Crafting a distinct resume for each job application significantly enhances your likelihood of securing the position. Generic resumes lack the impact of a targeted one.

What is the process for terminating my subscription?

Alternatively, you can independently cancel your subscription by navigating to the "My Plan" section of your account dashboard.
